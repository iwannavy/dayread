# Two Friends, Two Study Styles
> Source: Dayread Original | Date: March 12, 2026
> Note: Original educational text for launch curriculum — Week 3 (Causes, Choices, Comparisons)

Mina and Jisu are close friends, but they study in very different ways.

Mina prefers to study alone because she concentrates better in a quiet space.

She usually sits at her desk with headphones on, and she reviews her notes more carefully when no one is around.

Jisu, on the other hand, learns faster through group discussion.

He believes that explaining ideas to others is more useful than reading them silently.

While Mina feels more comfortable working by herself, Jisu feels more energized in a busy study room.

Because their styles are so different, they sometimes disagree about the best way to prepare for exams.

However, they both agree that finding your own method is more important than copying someone else's.
