# A Desk by the Window
> Source: Dayread Original | Date: March 2026
> Note: Week 2 — Space, Place, and Description (difficulty 1)

There is a small desk by the window in the corner of the room.

On the desk, there are two books, a cup of tea, and a notebook with blue lines.

The window faces east, so morning light reaches the desk before anything else in the room.

When the sun is strong, warm squares appear on the open pages.

The chair in front of the desk is old but comfortable, with a soft cushion that has faded over time.

Behind the desk, there is a wooden shelf filled with folders and a few plants in small pots.

The room is quiet most of the day, except for the occasional sound of birds outside.

In the afternoon, shadows move slowly across the wall, and the light turns from white to gold.

This desk is not special in any obvious way, but it is the place where one person starts every morning with a clear mind.

A good workspace does not need to be large; it only needs to feel ready.
